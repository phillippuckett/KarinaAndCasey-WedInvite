The Bride



Karina was born in São Paulo Brazil, and is the fifth of eight children. She grew up in a town called Sorocaba - up until the age of fifteen when she moved with her family to Riverton, Utah. She attended and graduated from Riverton High School. After high school she studied at LDS Business College, as well as at the University of Utah where she graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Psychology. Karina now works as a Therapeutic Preschool Specialist for The Children’s Center as well as a sewing operator for Cotopaxi. She loves children, sewing, the great outdoors, singing, health and wellness, and lifting. After graduating college Karina moved back home with her parents, and started attending the Daybreak YSA ward, where she met and fell in love with Casey Clark.

The Groom



Casey was born in Clearwater, Florida as the first of five children. He moved to Suwanee, Georgia when he was ten years of age. While in Georgia he attended and graduated from Lanier High School, where he participated in athletics as a cheerleader. Upon turning eighteen Casey was called to serve a full-time mission in the Ogden and Logan, Utah Mission. While serving as a missionary his family moved to South Jordan, Utah. Casey loves lifting, sports, and eating. After fulfilling his mission Casey moved in with his parents in Daybreak and began to attend the local YSA ward, where he met and fell in love with Karina Sobreira.
